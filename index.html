Fundraising

Our operating expenses for 2018-19 are just over $27,000. In order to continue our current level of support for Mt Pilchuck, we need to continue our fundraising efforts.

Please know that we do not expect every family to participate in every fundraiser! Our hope is that every student would be able to do something to support our work. We think providing a variety of options is the best way to do that.

Below are some ways to participate in fundraising that supports this PTA:

Catalog Sale
(fall)		Students sell items from catalog to family and friends and we get a portion of the profits.
Miles For Miles Fun Run
(spring)		Supporters donate to encourage kids / in honor of our late P.E. teacher Tyler Miles
Silver Graphics
(spring)		Sales of custom items featuring your student's own art
Direct donationWrite a check (or donate online) and spend the rest of the year serenely ignoring every fundraising request!
Box Tops for EducationCollect Box Tops from items you already buy and send them in with your student.
Rewards programsMake Mt Pilchuck Elementary PTA your beneficiary on Amazon Smile, Fred Meyer Rewards, or United Way.

A final word -- regarding SPAM

Emails provided to us will be added to our private general email list.

They are never sold or used for any purpose other than communicating information, including volunteer opportunities, to you.

We are committed to using BCC and other tools to protect your privacy.

Your email will be permanently removed from our list at your request.
